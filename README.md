# 🚀 GenAI-Impact-DS

**Exploring the Impact of Generative AI on Data Science Workflows (2020–2025)**

---

## 📌 Project Overview

**GenAI-Impact-DS** explores how **Generative AI (GenAI)** has reshaped **Data Science workflows** between **2020 and 2025**.

Using **real survey data**,that will be shared in this repository with the analysis itself, the main goal of project is analyzes trends, tools, and methodological shifts driven by the adoption of GenAI technologies such as **Large Language Models (LLMs)**, **code assistants**, and **automated analytics tools**.

The goal is to uncover **patterns in AI-driven work**, understand **evolving professional practices**, and assess how **GenAI is transforming the role of data scientists**.

Survey Link: https://forms.gle/CMPXVNPpFkr5kJtRA

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- 📊 Analyze changes in Data Science workflows before and after GenAI adoption  
- 🤖 Identify the most influential GenAI tools used by data professionals  
- ⏱️ Measure productivity, automation, and decision-making shifts  
- 🧠 Understand how GenAI affects skills, roles, and daily tasks  
- 🔮 Explore future trends in AI-augmented Data Science  

---

## 🧩 Research Questions

- How has Generative AI changed the way data scientists work?
- Which stages of the Data Science lifecycle are most impacted  
  *(EDA, modeling, deployment, communication)*?
- What tools are most widely adopted *(e.g., ChatGPT, GitHub Copilot, AutoML)*?
- Does GenAI improve productivity, creativity, or accuracy?
- What new risks, challenges, or dependencies emerge?

---

## 📊 Scope of Analysis

- **Time Period:** 2020–2025  
- **Audience:** Data Scientists, Analysts, ML Engineers  
- **Data Type:** Survey-based quantitative and qualitative data  

---

## 🔍 Key Themes

- Workflow automation and augmentation
- AI-assisted coding and analysis
- Skill evolution and role transformation
- Productivity gains vs. cognitive dependency
- Ethical and operational challenges

---

## 🔮 Expected Insights

- Increased automation of repetitive DS tasks
- Shift toward higher-level analytical and strategic work
- Growing reliance on GenAI tools for ideation and coding
- Emergence of new risks such as over-reliance and hallucinations
